Engineering notes from building a flight-ready distributed Data Acquisition System for rocket engines.

The system — internally called astrobase — uses multiple acquisition boards (TI AM263x + AD7606C ADCs) connected over TSN Ethernet, producing IRIG-compliant telemetry for flight applications.

These posts follow the engineering journey: the problems, the wrong turns, and the things that eventually worked.
